In-app purchases / Achats intégrés

HexMinefield 3D may offer an optional Premium Pack through the platform store. Purchases and purchase restoration are handled by Google Play on Android and the App Store on iOS.

If you have a purchase or restore problem, contact support with the platform you used and the approximate purchase date. Do not send payment card details.
